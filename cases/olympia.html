IAP-0.1 Identity Anchor Continuity Lineage

IAP verifies identity, declared lineage, and a time-sequenced chain of signed commitments (often hashes of internal state). It does not verify behavior, semantic correctness, or prevent runtime compromise.

Olympia Case Study

Scenario

Olympia is a multi-agent coordination system managing several task agents,

a planing agent, coding agent, review agent and a research agent.

The challenge:

> How can we verify that the coordinating authority remains

> cryptographically continuous over time?

IAP anchors identity and declared commitments --- not behavioral

integrity.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

IAP Integration

Identity Anchor

Olympia derives a deterministic agent_id from its public key. The

Registry issues an Identity Anchor record.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Continuity

Olympia records coordination events internally. It computes a

deterministic root hash of recorded events and anchors it via Continuity

records.

This provides tamper-evidence for what Olympia declares --- assuming its

internal ledger integrity assumptions hold.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Upgrade or Key Change

If Olympia upgrades and rotates keys:

  • A new identity is created
  • A Lineage record links it to the prior identity

The new identity starts its own continuity chain. Observers verify

declared lineage relationships.

IAP does not enforce canonical authority. It provides structural

transparency.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

What IAP Guarantees

  • Coordinator identity persistence
  • Sequenced continuity commitments
  • Verifiable declared derivation across upgrades

What IAP Does Not Guarantee

  • Prevention of internal tampering
  • Enforcement of governance legitimacy
  • Automatic canonical status among forks

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Result

Olympia gains:

  • Transparent declared upgrades
  • Verifiable identity continuity
  • Observable lineage transitions
