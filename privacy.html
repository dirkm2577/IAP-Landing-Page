Privacy Policy

1. General Information

This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is processed in connection with the Identity Anchor Protocol (IAP) Registry operated by Dirk Mischke.

2. Hosting and Server Logs

The hosting provider automatically collects and stores server log data including:

  • IP address
  • date and time of access
  • requested resources
  • browser and operating system information

Processing is based on Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest) for operational security, stability, and troubleshooting.

3. Registry API Usage

Requests to the Registry API may be logged for security, fraud prevention, and system integrity purposes. No automated profiling or behavioral tracking is performed.

4. Payments (Stripe)

Payments may be processed via Stripe. Stripe may process personal data in accordance with its own privacy policy and applicable law. Stripe acts as an independent data controller.

5. Contact via Email

If you contact the Registry Operator via email, your data will be used solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

6. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics tools.

7. Data Retention

Server log data is retained only as long as necessary for security and operational purposes and then deleted.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of processing of your personal data in accordance with applicable law.
